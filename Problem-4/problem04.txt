A String said to be a special string if either of two condition is met
1) all of the character are the same Ex. aaa
2) all characters except the middle one are the same Ex. aaabaaa

A special substring is any substring of a string which meets one of above criteria,find out how many special substrings can be formed form it.don't use collection data-type[list,set,map..etc]

Example

str=mnonopoo
Answer=12
